Welcome

Welcome to Nakake Secondary School. This is a Government school established in 2008 under the leadership of HM Reonard B. Rwazo.

The name "Nakake" comes from nearby villages: "N" from Nyakatuntu, "Ka" from Kasoni, and "Ke" from Kyerere.
